What we do

We offer a wide range of exciting programmes for local 11-18 year olds. Young people can gain Open College Network accreditations in Entry 3, Level 1 and Level 2, accredited by Ofqual, on the programmes listed below. These qualifications are designed to provide learners with the skills and knowledge necessary for various career paths. Many of our learners go on to find work related to the skills gained in our workshops.
We also offer one-to-one mentoring support and tuition in Entry levels and Functional skills in English and Maths.
